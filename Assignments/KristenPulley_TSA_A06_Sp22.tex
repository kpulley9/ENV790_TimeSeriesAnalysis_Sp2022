\hypertarget{q1}{%
\subsection{Q1}\label{q1}}

Describe the important characteristics of the sample autocorrelation
function (ACF) plot and the partial sample autocorrelation function
(PACF) plot for the following models:

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]

\item AR(2)

> Answer: An autoregressive model's current values depeend on its own previous values. The ACF for an autoregressive model shows a slow decay. The PACF gives the lag of the model.  

\item MA(1)

> Answer: A moving average model's current deviation from mean depends on previous deviation. The ACF decay usuallly cuts off at the begining and gives the lag. The PACF shows decay.

\end{enumerate}
